Katazome is a Japanese method of resist-dyeing fabric using very fine paper stencils, katagami, traditionally cut by hand. Numerically-generated images on dyed fabrics are made possible by laser-cutting the katagami.

Adaptability of the Materials of the Japanese Traditional Dyeing Paper Stencils for Producing with a Laser Cutter by Mikako Okita and Toshiaki Takesue is an excellent resource for learning about the history of this craft as well as adapting it for a laser cutter.
